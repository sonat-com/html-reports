Answering the same question over and over? Your help center can do that for you.

A help article that actually deflects tickets comes down to four moves:
1. Title it with the exact question customers ask — in their words.
2. Answer it in the first two sentences. No throat-clearing.
3. Show each step with a screenshot or short clip, not just text.
4. Publish it where people already search, then revise it from their feedback.
