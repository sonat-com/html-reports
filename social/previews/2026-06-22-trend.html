Only 14% of customer service issues are fully resolved in self-service today. That's the number from Gartner — and it might be lower than you expected.

The fix most teams reach for? More articles. But Gartner found that 43% of self-service failures happen because customers can't find the relevant content — not because it doesn't exist.

Structure is what moves the number: clear topic hierarchies, search that surfaces the right result, and docs written for the reader who knows nothing — not the writer who knows everything. That's where self-service actually breaks down.

If you're rebuilding your help center or knowledge base this year, start with structure before adding more content. → https://sonat.com
